Stay safe online

Let's take a look at what makes a weak password...
You should never use names like "Bob" or "Percy" as your password.
Personal information like your name and birthyear make a weak password.
Sports references are extremely common for passwords, and get cracked very quickly.
Four digit years like "2023" and "2009" should be avoided and predictable patterns like ABCDEFG or 123456.

Weak Passwords

  • Names arrow_forward "Bob"
  • Personal Info arrow_forward "Mark2009"
  • Sports References arrow_forward "6dunks"
  • Years arrow_forward "2023"
  • Predictable Patterns arrow_forward "123456"
