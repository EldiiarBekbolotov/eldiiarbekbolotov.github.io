Why Join?

Grow your skills as a programmer through many events, team projects, contests, and more. In the professional world, nearly all software is built in teams. Develop vital collaboration, interpersonal, time management, leadership skills that will help you get hired as a computer programmer. Make a lasting, meaningful impact on Khan Academy's computer programming community. All skill levels and programming languages are welcome! No matter your time commitment or geographical location, you can join and strive to improve your skills.

Frequently Asked Questions info

  • Question: Can anyone join Team Alpha?
  • Answer: Yes, anyone can join Team Alpha as long as they agree to be respectful and follow the Khan Academy community guidelines.
  • Question: Do I have to choose between joining another competition or team and this team?
  • Answer: No, any time commitment and time zone/geographical location is accepted.
  • Question: Is there a max team size?
  • Answer: No. We just opened up and everyone is welcome!
  • Question: Are programming teams allowed on Khan Academy?
  • Answer: Yes, provided that the team is for computer programming related topics only.
